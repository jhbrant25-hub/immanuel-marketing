I want to tell you about something I’ve been building.

Over the past several months, I’ve published a series of field manuals for Christian men — books that take the ancient theology of the Church Fathers, the Hebrew and Greek roots of Scripture, and the real spiritual battles men face every day, and put them into a formation curriculum built for the field.

Not a devotional. Not a motivational framework. A field manual.

These books are built around a single conviction: Christian men deserve formation, not just information. The enemy has specific weapons — worry, fear, control — and the ancient Church knew exactly what they were and how to defeat them.
